# Code of Conduct

ForceHub aims to be a professional, respectful, and practical project space.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept feedback when wording or behavior has an impact.
- Keep discussions focused on the project, documentation, and technical work.
- Respect privacy and do not share private data, credentials, logs, or sensitive environment details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information or sensitive operational details.
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, close, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that do not meet these expectations. Repeated or serious violations may result in blocked participation.
